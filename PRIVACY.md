# Nexus Calculator — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 23, 2026

Nexus Calculator ("the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

## TL;DR
Nexus Calculator does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. Everything runs on your device.

## Data we DO NOT collect
- Your name, email, phone number, or any contact information
- Your calculations, conversions, or history (stored locally on your device only)
- Your location
- Your device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Any personally identifiable information (PII)

## Data stored locally on your device
The app uses your device's `localStorage` to save:
- App settings (theme, sound, haptic, decimal places, voice language)
- Calculation history (last 100 calculations)
- Currency conversion presets
- Metric conversion presets

**This data never leaves your device.** You can clear it at any time via the **Settings → Reset All Data** button inside the app.

## Third-party services

### Live exchange rates (optional)
When you enable "Live Exchange Rates" in settings, the app fetches data anonymously from [open.er-api.com](https://www.exchangerate-api.com). The request contains no personal data — only a simple HTTPS GET for current rates. Disabling live rates disables this network request.

### Voice recognition
Voice commands use your device's built-in Speech Recognition API (provided by Google on Android, Apple on iOS, Microsoft on Windows). Audio is processed by your device's operating system, not by us. We never receive, store, or listen to your voice.

### Google Play Services
If you installed the app via Google Play Store, Google Play Services may collect standard diagnostic information per Google's privacy policy.

### Google AdMob (only if ads are enabled in a future version)
If a version of this app includes advertisements via Google AdMob, Google may collect your advertising ID and non-personal usage data to serve relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads on Android).

## Security
- All network requests use HTTPS
- No authentication or login is required
- No personal data is transmitted

## Your rights
Because we do not collect your data, there is no personal data for us to access, delete, or transfer. To remove any locally-stored data, use the **Reset All Data** button in the app's Settings panel or uninstall the app.

## Children's privacy
Nexus Calculator is rated "Everyone" and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted in the app's update notes.
